Lyricall - Smart Playlists for Spotify

Create Smart Playlists that automatically add songs that match your specified rules, each night at midnight.
Perfect for creating that playlist you always wanted to create, but never had the will to curate yourself.
Want have all your instrumental songs in one place? How about high energy songs? Or just songs by a collection of musicians?
Lyricall can do all of this for you.

Create a playlist that adds only intrumental songs from your favorite artist

Or just throw a bunch of random stuff in there and see what you get

Lyricall can listen on a variety of attributes, such as the simple:

  • Track Name
  • Available Markets
  • Disc Number
  • Track Number
  • Track Length
  • Explicitness
  • Popularity
  • Album Name
  • Album Label
  • Album Release Date
  • Artist Name

And the advanced:

  • Acousticness
  • Danceability
  • Energy
  • Instrumentalness
  • Key
  • Liveness
  • Loudness
  • Speechiness
  • Tempo
  • Time Signature
  • Valence
